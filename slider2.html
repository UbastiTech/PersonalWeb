Overview:

Divine Favour Community Based Organization is located at Embulbul Sub-location of Oloolua Location, Oloolua Ward, Ngong Division, Kajiado North District in Kajiado County.

We have been in existence since 2012, as an informal group, of women of course. In October 2016, the idea of formally registering it was born by the founder - Mary M. Watari




In May 2017, we registered with the relevant Ministry - Labour, Gender & Social Services - having 20 members who share the same vision, 14 women and 6 young men.




All members at DIFEP fit very well in the youth and young women programme - skills training for job creation, including:


  • Basic skills in cookery
  • Catering
  • Housekeeping
  • Computer Literacy
  • Small animals management & production

Divine Favour conducts the following programmes


  • Table Banking
  • Women & Girls' Empowerment (Social, Economic, Political, Spiritual)
  • Youth Strengthening & Capacity Building
  • Literacy for women & girls who otherwise either dropped at lower levels or never had an opportunity
  • Livelihood - Agriculture (Organic Farming); livestock (animal) - Production & Management

Business/ Project Summary

Project Title A: Table Banking
Click to Learn More

Divine Favour started table banking since 2012; every month we place our monthly contribution and loan repayments on the table, loaned to some members immediately as short term loans.

Table banking has been our main project as a source of income generation to our group - every month, we have a waiting list for people to be loaned.
